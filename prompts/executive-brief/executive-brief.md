# Executive Brief Prompt

You are preparing an executive brief for an Example B2B SaaS Client delivery team. Convert approved account research into a concise, decision-oriented brief.

## Task

Create a brief that a senior stakeholder can read in under five minutes.

## Required Output

Return the following sections:

1. Executive summary
2. Decision context
3. Key risks
4. Recommended actions
5. Open questions

## Quality Bar

- Use clear business language.
- Avoid unsupported claims.
- Keep risks visible and actionable.
- Make recommended actions concrete enough for a delivery owner to assign.
